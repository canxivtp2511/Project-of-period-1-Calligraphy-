Project Name

Vietnamese calligraphy originated from Chinese calligraphy, because during the Northern colonial period, the Han people propagated their traditional culture and writing into our country. Along with that, they also spread both art and aesthetic calligraphy into our country. Over a period of more than 1000 years of domination, the calligraphy of Han people has been developing very widely and popularly throughout our country. The first person in our country to use Chinese ink to write national script was Dong Ho poet, from that time, the calligraphy of national language began to appear. And he was also the person who spread and popularized the national calligraphy of the Vietnamese language to the ancient Vietnamese people.
